About Us

WHO WE ARE

Welcome to RSL Ref Solutions (Pvt) Ltd, your premier destination for Auto AC, Demestice AC solutions and spare parts. Since our establishment in 2022, we have positioned ourselves as an AC solution providing company committed to pushing the boundaries of innovation.

At the core of our mission is a fervent dedication to crafting inventive solutions that set our clients apart. Whether you're an entrepreneur, a government agency, or a large-scale corporate entity, our results-driven AC solutions cater to diverse needs.

At RSL we will do everything we can to make sure your issues are fully sorted as quickly as possible. We take care to have all the parts in our stock so you don't have to wait. Orders usually ship the same day they are received.

RSL has in stock most of the parts to fit vehicles from the 80's to the present. We import some of the parts directly when available. We have driers or accumulators to match as well as the expansion device for each make and model.

In addition to the widest selection of automotive air conditioning parts, RSL provides excellent customer service and technical support to all of our customers. If you need help troubleshooting a job feel free to call one of our techs who have over 20 years of automotive A/C experience.
